<p><p>The Inline X-Ray Inspection System market is categorized into two primary types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR utilizes digital detectors to capture high-resolution images of objects, enabling rapid inspection and analysis for quality assurance in manufacturing processes. In contrast, CT combines multiple X-ray images to create detailed 3D representations, allowing for comprehensive internal examination of complex components. Both technologies enhance accuracy and efficiency in detecting defects and ensuring product integrity in various industries.</p></p>
