<p><ul><li>Reserved Auction</li><li>Unreserved Auction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the online art auction market, there are two primary types: reserved and unreserved auctions. A reserved auction sets a minimum price that must be met for the artwork to be sold, protecting the seller’s interests. Conversely, in an unreserved auction, there is no minimum price, allowing items to be sold to the highest bidder regardless of the final amount. This can attract more bidders but may lead to lower selling prices for the seller.</p></p>
