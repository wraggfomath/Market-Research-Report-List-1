<p><p>The Electrochromic (EC) windows market comprises two primary types: Polymer Laminate EC and All-solid-state EC. Polymer Laminate EC utilizes layers of polymer materials, allowing for flexibility and lightweight characteristics, making them ideal for various architectural applications. In contrast, All-solid-state EC windows consist of solid materials without any liquid components, providing enhanced durability and stability. Both types effectively regulate light and heat transmission, contributing to energy efficiency and improving indoor comfort in commercial and residential spaces.</p></p>
