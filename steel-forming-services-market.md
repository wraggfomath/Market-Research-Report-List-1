<p><p>Steel forming services encompass various techniques for shaping steel into specific designs and applications. Roll forming involves continuously bending steel sheets or strips into desired profiles, ideal for producing long lengths of uniform cross-sections. Extrusion forming, on the other hand, involves forcing heated steel through a die to create complex shapes and profiles, often used for customized components. Both methods are essential in various industries, providing tailored solutions for construction, automotive, and manufacturing applications.</p></p>
