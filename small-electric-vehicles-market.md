BEVs operate solely on electric power, utilizing large battery packs, offering zero tailpipe emissions and lower operating costs. In contrast, PHEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, allowing drivers to operate on electric power for shorter trips and switch to gasoline for longer journeys. Both types cater to the growing demand for eco-friendly transportation solutions, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing carbon footprints.</p></p>
