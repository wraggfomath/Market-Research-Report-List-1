<p><p>The standard treadmill market comprises two main types: manual and motorized treadmills. Manual treadmills operate without electricity, relying on the user's movement to power the belt, making them cost-effective and portable. Motorized treadmills, on the other hand, feature an electric motor that drives the belt, offering various speed settings and typically equipped with advanced features like heart rate monitors and built-in workout programs. Each type caters to different user preferences and fitness goals, from casual walkers to serious runners.</p></p>
