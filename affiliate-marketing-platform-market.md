<p><p>Affiliate marketing platforms can be categorized based on their payment models. </p><p>1. Cost Per Sale (CPS) rewards affiliates when a sale is completed through their referral, incentivizing high conversion efforts. </p><p>2. Cost Per Lead (CPL) compensates affiliates for generating leads or sign-ups, focusing on building potential customer interest rather than immediate sales. </p><p>3. Cost Per Click (CPC) pays affiliates for each click on their referral link, driving traffic to the advertiser’s site, regardless of whether a sale or lead occurs.</p></p>
