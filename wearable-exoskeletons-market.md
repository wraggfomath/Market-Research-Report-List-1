<p><p>The wearable exoskeletons market is divided into two main types: full body and partial body exoskeletons. Full body exoskeletons provide comprehensive support to the entire body, enhancing mobility and strength, making them suitable for rehabilitation and heavy lifting tasks. In contrast, partial body exoskeletons target specific limbs or joints, improving the functionality of individual body parts, often used in rehabilitation or for assisting those with mobility impairments. Both types aim to enhance physical capabilities and support various applications.</p></p>
