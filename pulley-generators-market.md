<p><p>Pulley generators are classified primarily into single-wheel and two-wheel generators. Single-wheel generators utilize a single rotating pulley to convert kinetic energy into electrical energy, making them simpler and more compact, ideal for low-power applications. In contrast, two-wheel generators feature two pulleys, enhancing energy output and efficiency by capturing more kinetic energy. This design is suitable for larger applications requiring higher power generation. Both types cater to diverse market needs, balancing portability and energy demands.</p></p>
