<p><p>The uncooled infrared imaging equipment market comprises various types based on wavelength ranges. Near Infrared (NIR) focuses on short wavelengths, useful for applications like agriculture and security. Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) operates in slightly longer wavelengths, ideal for detecting moisture and certain materials. Mid-wave Infrared (MWIR) is suited for thermal imaging and surveillance, capturing temperature variations effectively. Long-wave Infrared (LWIR) refers to the longest wavelengths, typically employed in industrial, military, and environmental monitoring, as it detects heat emissions efficiently.</p></p>
