<p><p>The ostomy pouching system market is primarily divided into two categories: one-piece and two-piece pouching systems. One-piece systems integrate the pouch and adhesive barrier into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use, ideal for patients seeking a streamlined option. In contrast, two-piece systems feature a separate adhesive barrier and pouch, allowing for flexibility in changing pouches without removing the barrier. This versatility accommodates patients' varying lifestyle needs and preferences, enhancing comfort and confidence post-surgery.</p></p>
