<p><p>The low leakage diodes market comprises primarily two types: Monolithic Low Leakage Diodes and Monolithic Dual Channel Low Leakage Diodes. Monolithic Low Leakage Diodes are designed to minimize leakage current in single-channel applications, enhancing efficiency in sensitive electronic devices. Meanwhile, Monolithic Dual Channel Low Leakage Diodes integrate two channels on a single chip, providing versatility for more complex circuits while maintaining low leakage performance. Both types cater to the increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions in various electronic applications.</p></p>
