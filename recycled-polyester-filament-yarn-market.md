<p><p>Recycled polyester filament yarn is categorized into two main types: solid fiber and hollow fiber. Solid fiber yarn is dense and strong, making it ideal for applications requiring durability, such as textiles and industrial fabrics. In contrast, hollow fiber yarn features a tubular structure that offers lightweight properties and increased insulation, making it suitable for apparel, bedding, and outdoor gear. Both types contribute to sustainable practices by reducing waste and minimizing dependency on virgin polyester materials.</p></p>
