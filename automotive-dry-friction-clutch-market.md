<p><ul><li>Single Plate Dry Clutch</li><li>Multi Plate Dry Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dry friction clutch market is primarily segmented into single plate and multi plate dry clutches. Single plate dry clutches consist of one friction disc and are commonly used in lighter vehicles due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multi plate dry clutches feature multiple friction discs, allowing for greater torque capacity and smoother engagement, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles and applications such as motorcycles and racing cars. Both types facilitate efficient power transfer in vehicles.</p></p>
