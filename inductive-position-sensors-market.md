<p><p>Inductive position sensors operate based on electromagnetic principles to detect the position of metallic objects. The market for these sensors includes several types: </p><p>1. **Cylinder Sensors** are typically used in applications where space is limited, providing accurate measurements in rounded configurations.</p><p>2. **Rectangular Sensors** offer versatility in industrial environments with flat surfaces.</p><p>3. **Ring & Slot Sensors** are ideal for applications needing circular detection areas, suitable for rotary movements. </p><p>4. **Tubular Sensors** are designed for installation in cylindrical spaces, often utilized in hydraulic and pneumatic systems for real-time position feedback.</p></p>
