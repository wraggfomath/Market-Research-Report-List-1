<p><p>The GMP biologics market encompasses two primary types of antibodies: monoclonal antibodies and polyclonal antibodies. Monoclonal antibodies are engineered to target specific antigens, offering high specificity and consistent performance, making them valuable in diagnostics and therapeutics. In contrast, polyclonal antibodies are derived from multiple B-cell lines, recognizing various epitopes, which enhances their effectiveness in broad applications such as research and vaccine development. Both antibody types play crucial roles in advancing biotechnology and improving patient outcomes in various diseases.</p></p>
