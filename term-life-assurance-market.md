<p><ul><li>Renewable Term Life Insurance</li><li>Return Premium Term life insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Term life assurance refers to policies that provide coverage for a specified period. Renewable term life insurance allows policyholders to extend their coverage at the end of the term without needing to provide evidence of insurability. This is beneficial for those wanting flexibility as their needs change. Return premium term life insurance, on the other hand, refunds the premiums paid if the policyholder outlives the term, providing a safety net and encouraging individuals to invest in coverage with guaranteed returns.</p></p>
