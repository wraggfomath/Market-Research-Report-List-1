<p><p>The Contact Image Sensor (CIS) market comprises two primary types: monochrome and color contact image sensors. Monochrome CIS captures images in grayscale, making it ideal for applications requiring high resolution and clarity in black-and-white images, such as document scanning and barcode reading. In contrast, color contact image sensors are designed to capture full-color images, making them suitable for photographic applications, scanning of colored documents, and multimedia production. Both sensor types have distinct functionalities tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>
