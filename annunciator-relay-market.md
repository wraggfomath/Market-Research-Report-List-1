<p><p>The annunciator relay market is segmented into three main types: current relays, voltage relays, and power direction relays. Current relays monitor electrical current levels, ensuring they remain within safe limits and providing alerts for anomalies. Voltage relays focus on voltage levels, triggering alarms for over or under-voltage conditions to protect electrical systems. Power direction relays determine the flow of power in a circuit, ensuring proper operation in applications like generator synchronization and fault detection, enhancing system reliability and safety.</p></p>
