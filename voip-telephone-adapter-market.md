<p><p>The VoIP telephone adapter market is primarily categorized into two types: single-port and multi-port adapters. Single-port adapters connect one analog telephone to a VoIP network, providing a straightforward solution for individual users or small offices. In contrast, multi-port adapters support multiple analog phones, making them ideal for larger businesses that require several lines. Both types facilitate the transition from traditional telephony to VoIP, offering flexibility and scalable communication solutions tailored to different user needs or organizational sizes.</p></p>
