<p><p>Movie ticketing systems are categorized into on-premise and cloud-based markets. On-premise systems are installed locally within a cinema's infrastructure, offering control over data and operations but requiring significant upfront investments in hardware and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems operate online, allowing theaters to manage ticketing through a web interface. They offer scalability, lower initial costs, and ease of access from any device, making them a popular choice for cinemas looking to streamline operations and enhance customer experience.</p></p>
