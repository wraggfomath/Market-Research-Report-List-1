<p><p>The Controlled Atmosphere Furnaces market is categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical furnaces. Horizontal type furnaces are designed for more extensive thermal processing, providing uniform temperature distribution and easier loading and unloading of materials. In contrast, vertical type furnaces occupy less floor space and are suitable for processes where materials require a continuous flow. Both types are used in various applications, including metal heat treatment, sintering, and ceramics, ensuring precise control over the environment during processing.</p></p>
