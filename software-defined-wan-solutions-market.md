<p><p>Software-Defined WAN (SD-WAN) solutions can be categorized into three market types: virtual appliances, physical appliances, and hybrid models. </p><p>Virtual appliances leverage software to run on existing hardware, providing flexibility and scalability. Physical appliances, on the other hand, are dedicated hardware devices that deliver optimized network performance. Hybrid solutions combine both virtual and physical elements, allowing organizations to transition smoothly between on-premises and cloud environments. This diversity enables businesses to tailor their WAN deployments to specific needs and operational contexts.</p></p>
