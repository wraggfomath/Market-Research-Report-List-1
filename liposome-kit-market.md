<p><p>The liposome kit market is primarily categorized into two types: plain liposomes and lyophilized liposomes. Plain liposomes are typically composed of lipid bilayers that encapsulate drugs, facilitating targeted delivery and improving bioavailability. They are commonly used in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and research applications. Lyophilized liposomes, on the other hand, are freeze-dried forms that enhance stability and shelf life, making them easier to store and transport. Both types play crucial roles in drug formulation and delivery systems.</p></p>
